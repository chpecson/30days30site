Filipino dishes

Some of the delicious foods of the filipinos

Pancit

In Filipino cuisine, pancit or pansit are noodles. Noodles were introduced into the Philippines by the Chinese and have since been adopted into local cuisine.

Sinigang

Sinigang is a Filipino soup or stew characterized by its sour and savoury taste most often associated with tamarind. It is one of the more popular viands in Philippine cuisine, and is related to the Malaysian dish singgang.
